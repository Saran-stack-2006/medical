Gemer 2 Tablet Details

Gemer 2 Tablet is a combination of Glimepiride and Metformin, used to control high blood sugar levels in patients with type 2 diabetes. It improves the body's response to insulin and reduces sugar production in the liver.

Key Benefits:

  • Improves insulin sensitivity
  • Decreases sugar production in liver
  • Helps prevent complications like kidney damage and heart issues
